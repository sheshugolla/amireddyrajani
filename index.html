About Dr. Rajani

Dr. Amireddy Rajani is a distinguished social activist and human rights defender who has dedicated her life to empowering marginalized communities, particularly women and persons with disabilities.

With a doctorate in Social Work and over 15 years of grassroots experience, she combines legal expertise with compassionate counseling. Her approach is holistic - addressing not just legal battles, but rebuilding lives, restoring dignity, and ensuring long-term support.

Operating from Krishna District, her organization's reach extends across Andhra Pradesh and neighboring states, providing free legal aid, family mediation, and government scheme facilitation to thousands of families annually.

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your services free?

Yes, basic consultation and counseling are completely free. For complex legal cases requiring court representation, we charge minimal fees.

How confidential is my information?

Absolute confidentiality is maintained. All case details are protected under professional ethics. We never disclose client information without explicit consent.

Do I need appointment or can I walk in?

Both options available. For emergency situations, immediate walk-in support is provided. For detailed consultations, prior appointment ensures dedicated time.

What documents should I bring?

Bring any relevant documents: marriage certificate, Aadhaar, property papers, previous court orders, medical reports, police complaints, etc.
